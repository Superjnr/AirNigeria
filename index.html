Air Nigeria


Nigeria Air is a revolutionary airline that will change the course of air travels across Nigeria, Ghana and the rest of the world in years to come. Air Nigeria plans to establish itself as a niche player in growing air travels and cargo markets linking all the cities across Nigeria and other countries in Africa. By focusing passionately on the needs of travellers and cargo services, Nigerian Air will provide the best value proposition in the markets it serves.

Air Nigeria headquarters is located in Lagos and will roll out its services across all of Nigeria , including Abuja, Uyo and smaller cities like Calabar in the first quarter of 2023. By 2024, it is projected to extend its services world wide. It will offer customers a high level of services and comfort at 50 percent of what is obtainable in the market today.
